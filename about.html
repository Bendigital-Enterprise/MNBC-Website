About Us

Meanwood Ndeke Baptist Church is a transformational church that is committed to maturing (perfecting) believers to the measure of the fullness of Christ. We are a Baptist Church that holds on to the Reformed Faith as summarized in the 1689 Baptist Confession of Faith. We endeavor therefore to welcome non-believers, evangelize to them, assimilate them as new believers into our church membership, nurture them to maturity, meet their needs, and equip them to be ministers in the Church.

Our Philosophy

Mission Statement

To glorify God by being a Transformational Church that in love evangelizes non-believers and assimilates them as new believers into church membership for their nurturing into spiritual maturity and training them to be ministers of Christ in the Church.

Vision

A teaching, equipping and reforming Church.

Motto

"Building a Community for God"

Core Values

  • A high view of God
  • Lordship of Christ Jesus
  • Essentiality of faith and grace in salvation
  • Ministry to Believers
  • Ministry to Non-believers
  • Absolute Authority of Scripture
  • Sound Doctrine
  • Personal Holiness
  • Spiritual (or Biblical) Authority
  • Biblical Fellowship
  • Biblical Stewardship
